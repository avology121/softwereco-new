SOFTWARE TESTING

Functional testing.

The secret behind the quality of our services is our team of experienced, trained and ISTQB certified testers. Our testers can handle any job by conducting exploratory testing (independently learning and discovering the functions of the application), or by following a prepared test scenario.

The final result of our cooperation will be a report containing all the errors in the application. Errors will be grouped and arranged according to the scale of importance. We will tell you which of the detected errors should be remedied first, to create a positive user experience and further develop the digital product or service

E-COMMERCE

We build gorgeous
online stores.

Mobile and web applications are playing an increasingly major role in how companies interact with their users. In turn, applications are the closest to the client and their data. During security testing, both server and application components are examined.

Penetration tests will be conducted in the following areas:

  • Improper Platform Usage
  • Insecure Data Storage
  • Insecure Communication
  • Insecure Authentication and Authorisation
  • Insufficient Cryptography
  • Client Code Quality & Code Tampering
  • Reverse Engineering & Extraneous Functionality
